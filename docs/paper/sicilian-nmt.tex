\paragraph{Relation to prior published numbers.}
Wdowiak reports (on his own, in-domain, hand-selected test set, with the full recipe)
En$\rightarrow$Sc/Sc$\rightarrow$En BLEU of 25.1/29.1 (baseline), 35.0/36.8 (augmented),
and 45.1/48.6 (Reverse-Training). These are \emph{not} a head-to-head with ours: different
test sets and pipelines. A fair comparison requires running one system on the other's test
set, or reproducing the full recipe on our data --- the goal of the next experiments.
